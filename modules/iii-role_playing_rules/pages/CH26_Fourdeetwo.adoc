= Four Dee Two System
Four dice rolled by two players.
It is a competitive roll system.
It replaces the following rolls.
. Attack Rolls
. Performance Rolls
. Attribute Rolls
. Etcetera

== Four D Two Result
.Persona Effect = 2d6 - 2d6
****
Generates a range from +10 to -10. Positive is positive. Negative is negative.
****
